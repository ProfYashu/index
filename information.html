IRRIGATION SYSTEM

Irrigation is the artificial application of water to land for agricultural, horticultural, or recreational purposes. It is a crucial component of modern agriculture, enabling farmers to grow crops in areas with limited rainfall or to supplement natural rainfall.

LAND PREPARATION

Land preparation is a crucial step in agriculture, involving the process of clearing, breaking up, and modifying the soil to create favorable conditions for crop growth. The goal of land preparation is to develop the potential of the land, ensuring optimal tree growth, survival, and uniformity of the crop.

PESTICIDES

Pesticides are substances or mixtures of substances used in agriculture to control a wide range of pests and disease carriers, including mosquitoes, ticks, rats, and mice. In agriculture, pesticides are used to control weeds, insect infestations, and diseases
